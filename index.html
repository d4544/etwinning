What is eTwinning?

eTwinning is the European Commission's free, safe online platform that connects schools across Europe and beyond. Teachers and students collaborate on projects using digital tools, develop key skills, and build real intercultural friendships.

Part of the European School Education Platform, it’s used by over 1 million teachers and students every year.

Our Project: The Power of S.H.E.

A year-long international eTwinning project (September 2025 – May 2026) for secondary school students.

We explore women’s rights, activism, and representation in cinema and beyond. Students work in international teams to research, create digital stories, produce short videos, and discuss gender issues through the lens of film.

Building on two successful years of similar projects (including work around the film Suffragettes), we continue improving every year with fresh ideas and stronger collaboration.
